Septimius Capital

Investing from the Bottom Top

Septimius Capital, LLC is an early-stage investment company empowering entrepreneurs, businesses and communities across Africa. The company was founded to invest with companies, partnerships, entrepreneurs and other founders whose ideas, business models and products aligns with the company values and ethos.

Septimius Capital got its name from the first and only black Roman Emperor. We are Septimius due to our early-stage strategy to not only incubate great ideas but also become the first investor in those entrepreneurs and businesses. We invest in people with understanding of their industries, and impactful leaders capable of scaling companies by being result and solution driven through problem solving.

2010

Our Story

Our story begin in 2010 with our first investment into a small start-up that teaches SAP modules such as business intelligence, business warehousing and tech to individuals and introduce those individuals to big companies and government run enterprises in Africa. After that, we set out to bring venture capital to Africa and African around the world. Our initial investment in that firm was $1,600. United States dollars.

Africa

It's time that African begin to create the companies that will drive our economy. At Septimius Capital, we are here to partner with those individuals on every level. An intermediary to bridge the gap in financing for entrepreneurs across Africa.
