    Capital & Credit:

    The Ventures Credit plays a pivotal role by serving as debt or equity investment that either functions similar to a bank loan (debt) or private equity by taking an equity position. Whether debt or equity, we serve as partners to our investees/founders and entrepreneurs by leveraging our experiences across many sectors and industries to offer strategic advisory services.

    The service is part of our core consulting services which also extends to investee on the small to medium size enterprises. These complimentary services will come in the form of strategies that will streamline their businesses and improve profitability. We will create business advisory centers that serve as incubators that will be accessible to all of our small business borrower and partners who need help developing and growing their business. The strategy for the fund is to create an investment vehicle that focuses on micro, small and medium sized enterprises while remaining core to its micro partners focus.

    Investment Initiatives

    • Investing in women and small traders
    • Driving economic growth across Africa
    • Creating an investment and lending intermediary that combines strategy and creative financial solutions.
    • Offering robust advisory services to partnered companies, entrepreneurs, and founders.
    • Creating jobs in the economy
    • Building early entrepreneurial skillset in youth
