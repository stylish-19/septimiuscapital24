Africa

Investing from the Bottom Top

In Africa, we have a broader strategy that is inclusive of many aspects of investing. We focus on early stage investing in micro, small and medium sized enterprises. We also have a fund that focuses solely on market women (Market Women Fund) as they are the engine of many of the small shops in various markets. We invest via debt or equity in businesses and early-stage entrepreneurs and businesses. Our investees have as an added service, a unique complementary advisory (Business As a Service â€“ BAS) as a way to help them understand strategy. This is our way of saying; we are here whenever you need us.

To continue the business ecosystem, we have pitch event around the country in addition to our financial literacy component which incubates ideas. We are building an investment firm from the bottom up that is inclusive in its areas of focus with a financial literacy through our incubation to develop great products and services capable of being scaled beyond Liberia.

Our Real Estate Strategy

We seek to invest in rising opportunities in the real estate sector in the US, Liberia, and other parts of Africa. Through direct investments and partnerships, we aim to develop and invest in residential, retail spaces, business offices, low- and moderate-income houses, residential communities, and commercial real estate.

Our team brings extensive experience in structuring real estate financial transactions, leveraging developmental finance, and utilizing special economic zones to develop and finance the real estate sector.
