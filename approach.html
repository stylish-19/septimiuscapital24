Investment Guidelines

These investment guidelines help Septimius Capital identify the right companies with the potential to scale commercially while creating social impact. Septimius Capital seeks to invest in companies where our investment is key to scaling the business and where our investment does not represent more than 50% of the company total capital.

Investment Criteria

Stage of Company

Septimius Capital seeks to invest in micro start-ups and existing companies with potential to create impact in their various sectors - technology, agriculture women owned businesses, partnerships in real estate and various other sectors that align with our investment ethos.

Strong Management Team

We look for companies with competent management team that understand their market and sector. As much as we are going to work tirelessly, we look for a team that is committed to their companies with such commitment. Consistency in business profits, financials and customer relations. We look to invest in companies that understand their supply chain and distribution channels. However, we can also help guiding your business to scale...this is why are a unique partner.

Due Deligence

We will spend some time shadowing potential businesses to get a clear understanding of their operations. During our due diligence, we are looking for key performance indexes or indicators (KPI) that drives growth in your company.

Investment Process

The investment process is a systematic approach to managing and allocating assets with the goal of achieving financial objectives.

Incubation

Early stage of developing and nurturing ideas, projects, or organisms in a controlled environment.

Deal Identification

Finding and evaluating potential business opportunities or investment targets.

Investment Analysis

Ability to scale and offer value added creation

Decision to Invest

contingent on a favorable out of our investment analysis

Scale to value

Partnering with the founder and or board to advise and help scale the company or ides.

Divest Process

We exit when the company is up to scale and firing on all cylinders of growth.
